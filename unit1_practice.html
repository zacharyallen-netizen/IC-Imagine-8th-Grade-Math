🔍 Common Mistakes

Can you spot what went wrong?

Classify: 0.333...
✗ Student: Student says: Irrational (it goes on forever)
✓ Fix: WRONG! Repeating decimals ARE rational. 0.333... = 1/3
💡 Irrational means non-repeating AND non-terminating.
Estimate √20
✗ Student: Student says: √20 ≈ 10 (because 20/2 = 10)
✓ Fix: WRONG! You don't divide by 2. √20 is between √16=4 and √25=5, so ≈ 4.5
💡 Find the perfect squares AROUND the number, not divide.

⚡ Key Tips for This Unit

✅ All fractions are rational — even ugly ones like 17/31.
✅ π is NOT 3.14 — 3.14 is rational. π itself is irrational.
✅ If the square root is not a perfect square, it's irrational.
✅ Memorize perfect squares 1-144: 1,4,9,16,25,36,49,64,81,100,121,144
